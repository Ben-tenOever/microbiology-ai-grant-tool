How to use this tool

  1. Check your profile. Visit the Faculty Profiles page, search for your name, and confirm that your synopsis, capabilities, and focus areas look reasonable.
  2. Bring a question or call. Start with either (a) a Grants.gov , or (b) a scientific question you want to build a team around.
  3. Use the Team Builder. Try the preview tool to see how future grant-matching will work once UltraViolet AI is integrated.
  4. Refine and reality-check. Adjust the suggested teams based on bandwidth, trainee availability, models, and existing collaborations.
  5. Decide next steps. If a proposed project looks promising, schedule a small-group meeting to refine hypotheses, division of labor, and a submission timeline.
Suggestions and corrections

If you spot errors in your profile or have ideas for better clustering or team suggestions, please send edits to the Chair so the master spreadsheet and this tool can be updated. The aim is to support the department, not to lock anyone into a static description.
